⚖️ B01 — AI Compliance Checker

RAG-powered compliance assessment tool for the EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689) and GDPR (Regulation 2016/679). Classifies AI systems by risk tier, retrieves relevant regulation articles via FAISS, and generates actionable compliance checklists using Claude 3.5 Sonnet.

Confidence Score Design

Retrieval confidence is derived from the FAISS L2 distance of the best-matching article chunk. Legal text retrieval inherently produces higher distances than general text — confidence thresholds are calibrated accordingly.

🟢 High confidence (≥50%)Strong article match
🟡 Medium confidence (25–50%)Typical legal retrieval
🔴 Low confidence (<25%)Review citations manually
Note: Medium confidence (25–50%) is normal for legal text and does not indicate incorrect classification. The cited articles and Claude's reasoning should be evaluated independently of the confidence score.
Key Design Decisions
Article-boundary chunking: The EU AI Act and GDPR are split at Article boundaries rather than fixed token sizes. This preserves legal semantic units — cross-referencing between sub-clauses within an article is not broken by arbitrary token splits. Result: 222 EU AI Act chunks and 161 GDPR chunks.
Local embeddings, zero indexing cost: all-MiniLM-L6-v2 runs entirely on CPU via sentence-transformers. Building both FAISS indexes from scratch takes under 20 seconds and costs zero API credits. Only Claude API calls consume credits.
Dual-regulation architecture: EU AI Act and GDPR are indexed separately in independent FAISS stores. The "Both" mode retrieves top-4 chunks from each index and merges them into a single prompt with regulation-labelled sections, allowing Claude to reason about overlapping obligations simultaneously.
Structured output prompt: The system prompt enforces a fixed response schema (RISK TIER → REASONING → CITED ARTICLES → CHECKLIST → CAVEATS). This ensures consistent, parseable output for UI badge rendering and prevents Claude from producing free-form prose that the frontend cannot process.

Limitations & Honest Caveats
LimitationDetail
Indicative only Not a substitute for qualified legal advice. Compliance decisions require specialist review.
Coverage gap 383 chunks cover core articles. Recitals, annexes, and implementing acts are partially indexed.
Jurisdiction Assumes EU deployment. Member state implementing legislation is not included in the knowledge base.
Regulation currency EU AI Act as published August 2024. GDPR as of 2018. Delegated acts and guidance not indexed.
Confidence calibration L2 distance thresholds are heuristic. Low confidence does not mean incorrect classification.
